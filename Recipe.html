KASHMIRI MIRCH KI CHUTNEY

This is an unbeliveably tasty chutney recipe.
Goes with almost every thing

Recipe

Ingredients

  1. kashmiri red chilli 4 large
  2. Tomato 2 medium chopped
  3. Cumin seeds 1tsp
  4. Garlic 3-4 cloves
  5. salt 1tsp

Directions

  • soak kashmiri lal mirch in water for 1 hour
  • cut kashmiri lal mirch with the help of kitchen scissor
  • ln grinder add kashmiri lal mirch,tomato,cumin seeds,garlic cloves,salt and blend them properly to make a fine paste
  • chutney is ready
